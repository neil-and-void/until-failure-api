Getting Started

Welcome to the Until Failure support page! Here, you'll find all the information you need to get started with our app.

Download and Install

To download and install Until Failure, follow these steps:

  1. Visit the App Store page for Until Failure.
  2. Click the "Download" button.
  3. Follow the prompts to install the app on your device.

Create an Account

To create an account in Until Failure, follow these steps:

  1. Open the app on your device.
  2. Tap "Sign Up" and enter your details.
  3. Set a password for your account.
  4. Verify your email address

Contact Us

If you have any questions or issues with Until Failure, please contact us and we'll be happy to assist you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about Until Failure:

  1. How do I reset my password?

    To reset your password, tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen and follow the prompts.

  2. Can I use the app on multiple devices?

    Yes, you can use the app on multiple devices by logging in with the same account on each device.

  3. How do I delete my account?

    Login to Until Failure and go to profile. There should be a cog wheel at the top right. Tapping that should give you the option to delete your account.
