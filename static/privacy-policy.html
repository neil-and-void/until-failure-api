Privacy Policy

Thank you for considering our workout journal app! We take your privacy very seriously, and we want you to feel comfortable using our app. This privacy policy explains how we collect and use your information.

Information We Collect:

When you create an account with our app, we collect your email address. This email address is used to create and verify your account, and to send you important updates and notifications related to our app. We also collect your email address when you reset your password through our app.

How We Use Your Information:

We use your email address to create and verify your account, and to send you important updates and notifications related to our app. We do not share your email address with any third parties. We do not use your email address for any marketing purposes. We do not sell your information to anyone.

Data Security:

We take data security very seriously. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your email address and other information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We take reasonable steps to ensure that your information is stored securely and that it is only accessible by authorized personnel.

Your Rights:

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your information at any time. You can do this by logging into your account and making the necessary changes. If you have any questions or concerns about your information, you can contact us at any time.

Changes to this Policy:

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. If we make any material changes to this policy, we will notify you by email or by posting a notice on our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

Thank you for trusting us with your information. If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact us.
